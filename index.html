Project

Electric Propulsion System for CubeSats

Download Project Report

Design and analysis of a Pulsed Plasma Thruster(PPT) integrated into a 3U CubeSat. CubeSat's lack a propulsive device, therefore their mission life is limited. We can extend the lifetime of CubeSats by using thrusters, which can also be employed to maintain the orbit and increase its orbit.

Modal analysis and Random vibrations testing were performed on the CubeSat frame and with the thrusters mounted inside CubeSat. There are a total of 8 thrusters that are used to control and also achieve three-axis control.

Power Analysis (Solar Panel groups)

Solar Panel tool in STK

Solar panel power profile at 20 degree inclination of CubeSat

The maximum power produced by all the solar panels was around 9.117 (W), the solar panels Minus X and Minus Y produced the most power compared to the other two panel groups. The maximum power produced by the Minus Y panel was around 6.513 (W), whereas the Minus X panel produced maximum power of around 6.512 (W). The power produced by the Plus X panel was maximum at the beginning which was about 2.813 (W) but the power remained constant during the period where the panel was not eclipsed. The power range was about 2.813 (W) – 2.787 (W).
